What is Bathtub Reglazing?

Bathtub reglazing is a process that involves refinishing your old bathtub to restore its original luster and finish. This technique allows you to keep your existing tub while giving it a fresh new look. Whether your tub has scratches, stains, or discoloration, reglazing can effectively cover these imperfections and extend the life of your bathtub.
